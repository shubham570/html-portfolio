The Best Web-Series According to Me

My top 3 webseries of all-time

Mirzapur

A great web-series shown from uttar pradesh background with highly skilled actors.

Family Man

Really cool web-series shows how a family man works as an underagent for his country.

Kota Factory

Beautifully written script and a real one. This shows students hustle for their dream college and how they give their everything for this one paper.
